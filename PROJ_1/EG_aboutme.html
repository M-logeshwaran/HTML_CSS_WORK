ABOUT ME





* Hi, I’m Logesh Waran — the creator of this site. I’m an independent learner who recently started exploring the world of web development. I created this website to help gamers easily download classic emulator games and the required emulators all in one place.

* This is just the beginning. I plan to keep updating and improving this site as I learn more about web design and development. Thank you for visiting — and if you enjoy retro gaming, you’re in the right place!
